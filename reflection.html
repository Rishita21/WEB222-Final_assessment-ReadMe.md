Final Assessment - Reflection

---> Explain the concept of Responsive Design. What does the term mean?

  • A Responsive web design is something that we use to make the stuff look ethical on a number of screens by shrinking the document, enlarging it, hiding or moving etc in both html and css.
  • The term responsive design basically sets many great manners to make any layout respond to the device.
  • In layman’s language, we can define the responsive design as a GUI that helps the content of the screen to fit in the size of the screen properly and without any adjustments.

---> How does responsive design relate to web technologies?

  • In today’s world, almost each and every company wants their website to work properly on the screen of mobile phones also just like it works on the computer screen.
  • Also, sometimes being a website creator some things can be irritating while designing the webpage as there comes a vast difference in the laptop’s screen and in the mobile phone’s screen. In such scenarios, responsive design is proved to be a great alternative and solution to such problems.
  • An approach to webpage creation which is known to be responsive design relies on responsive layouts, flexible images and cascading style sheet media queries. The purpose of this responsive design is to detect the visitor’s screen size and orientation and according to it changes that particular layout.
  • Also as the days are passing the usage of mobile phones are inclining in the terms of internet. And also there are various types of applications which are included in the smartphones and such application always need a good webpage and an interface.

  • Designing a webpage is something that needs presence of mind as well as our creativity about how much good webpage we can create with our designing skills.
  • This web apps are divided into user-interface, front-end and the back-end. There are certain types of steps for the preparation of these interfaces.
  • Through my research, I learnt a varied areas in the matter of designing websites such as giving them appropriate background colours and images according to their genre, deciding the font-family and the writing and their size.
  • I also got a good hold on codes like validating that includes the alert messages that pops when the user does not enter in the way he is asked for.
  • Linking different pages, including images, including videos and the most important thing that is to design the interface visually and get it on the screen with the help of my skills is the most foremost thing to learn throughout the subject.
